The Grange

AUCKLAND

The Grange is more than just a gorgeously designed eatery - it’s a versatile, light-filled venue that outdoes
itself in presentation, service and more. We’re committed to bringing you an experience like none other, catering to cocktail
parties, private dinners, lunches and soirées.

Tree Church

Hamilton

The TreeChurch, Ohaupo, is a unique and beautiful venue for weddings and other special occasions. The TreeChurch is set in 2 hectares of beautiful gardens which are also open for tours and visitors.

Skyline

Queenstown

Embraced by one of New Zealand’s most iconic landscapes, Skyline Queenstown perches high above Lake Wakatipu, offering simply stunning views day or night. Accessed by scenic Gondola, Skyline is a truly unique venue for Queenstown – the journey is just as exciting as the destination. With decades of experience, you can relax safe in the knowledge that our team has everything in hand to make your next event an outstanding success.
