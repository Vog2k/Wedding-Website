Karikari Peninsula, Northisland

Experience a wedding venue like no other and make your special day an event to remember as you celebrate surrounded by the beauty and stunning scenery of the heart of Northland. Exchange your vows surrounded by the vines in our lovely vineyard, look out over sweeping views of the Pacific Ocean, and commemorate your day with photos taken against the backdrop of golden sands and deep blue sea. Indulge in casual dining with your guests in our winery’s barrel room and enjoy the Estate’s accommodation without having to leave your wedding venue. Ohaupo Hamilton

Ask a local Hamiltonian the number one thing you shouldn't miss in their city, and there's a high chance they'll say the Hamilton Gardens.While traditional botanical gardens focus on types of plants and are organized accordingly, the Hamilton Gardens are laid out according to different kinds of garden design. The effect is a showcase of the cultural meaning of gardens over the centuries.

Mission House Drive, Pukawa, Lake Taupo

Taupo, a town near the centre of New Zealand's North Island, is distinguished by its lakefront setting and outdoor sports ranging from fishing to jet-boating. The vast waters of Lake Taupo, a volcanic caldera, drain into the Huka Falls, dramatic, crystal-blue cascades reachable by hikingh and biking trails. Surrounding Taupo are hot springs like those filling the Craters of the Moon reserve.
